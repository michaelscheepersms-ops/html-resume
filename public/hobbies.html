Learning First

If I had to name my favourite hobby, it would simply be learning. Everything else I love branches out from that. I do not only enjoy doing things. I enjoy understanding them, improving at them, and seeing how far the mind, body and spirit can go.

Learning Through the Body

Some of the things I love most are physical because they teach discipline, humility, patience and courage. Through movement, I learn about fear, effort, consistency and what the human body is capable of.

  • Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu
  • Gymnastics
  • Yoga
  • Ultra running
  • Movement and new skills- skills that require movement. Movement is everything.

Learning Through the Mind

I am deeply interested in the mind, how people think, how fear works, how habits are built, and how a person can keep growing.

  • Languages
  • Coding
  • The human mind
  • Fear and growth
  • Self-improvement
  • Life and meaning

Learning Through Other People

I love learning about other people, their lives, their struggles, their cultures and the way they see the world.

  • Food culture
  • Other people’s lives
  • Different cultures
  • Human behaviour
  • Life stories

Learning Through Faith

I love learning about God, about character and about how to live well. Faith shapes the way I see growth, purpose, discipline, relationships and life itself.

What All My Hobbies Have in Common

At the centre of all my hobbies is one thing: I want to understand life more deeply. Maybe I never will. Maybe I never become the person I want to be. Maybe I become more, maybe less. But it's the journey, or atleast thats my perspective now- March 2026.
